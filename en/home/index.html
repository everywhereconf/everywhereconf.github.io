About the event

What ?

The Multiplateform conference is fully dedicated to multiplateform solutions such as Flutter, React Native or Kotlin Multiplateforme.

Our purpose is to give all the answers you have regarding multiplatform topic.

Whether you wonder if you should learn multiplatform, how to begin or how experts see the future of multiplatform solutions, this conference is for you.

Where ?

The conference is 100% online and totally free.

May you want to talk with us or chat with some new friends, we booked a bar to enjoy the after party together. A small fee will be requested when buying your ticket.

When ?

The 27th of November, 2020
